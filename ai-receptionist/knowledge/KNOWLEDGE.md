## The Core Problem Epicware Solves

When someone searches "best dentist near me" or "salon in Tampines", Google shows the top 3 results. Those 3 get 90% of the clicks. Most local businesses lose customers daily because:

1. Fewer Google reviews than competitors → lower ranking
2. Incomplete Google Business Profile → missing keywords Google uses to rank
3. No consistent posting strategy → Google sees competitors as more active

These are all fixable. And the fix compounds over time — more reviews → higher ranking → more customers → more reviews.

## Products

### EpicReview — Reputation Management

What it does: Automates Google review collection using a QR code system at the point of service. Smart filter catches unhappy customers privately before they post publicly. Happy customers go straight to Google.

How it works (3 steps):
1. Customer scans a branded QR standee at the table or counter
2. Smart filter routes: happy → Google review, unhappy → private feedback
3. Spin-the-wheel reward makes reviewing fun and drives repeat visits

Dashboard shows: real-time review count, star rating trend, review velocity, 1-star interceptions.

Real result: Client went from 3.8 → 4.8 stars, avg +143 new reviews/month.

## What GEO Means (explain this clearly when asked)

GEO = Generative Engine Optimisation. It means getting a business cited in AI-generated answers — when someone asks ChatGPT "best dental clinic in Singapore" or searches on Perplexity or Google AI Overviews, Epicware gets the client's business cited in those answers.

This is different from traditional SEO (ranking in Google's blue links). GEO is available on Authority (1 keyword) and Domination (3 keywords) plans.
